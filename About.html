The Swedish tradition Fridaymyz (Fredagsmys) is activities when family or friends gather on Friday night to mark the end of the working week and celebrate the weekend. The snack consumed during the night is a very central, if not the most important part of the Fridaymyz. But what should you do when you're lacking fantasy or you and your buddy can’t agreee on what to eat? Life is full of hard choices - let’s not make the fridaymyz one of them.
